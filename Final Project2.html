YU-GI-OH! CHARACTERS

Yami Yugi

Yami Yugi's signature card is the "Dark Magician". This iconic card is one of the most powerful and central cards in his deck throughout the Yu-Gi-Oh! anime and manga series. The Dark Magician is often portrayed as a powerful wizard and serves as Yami Yugi's main card, symbolizing his strength and leadership in duels.

Jaden Yuki

Jaden Yuki's signature card in Yu-Gi-Oh! is the Elemental HERO Neos. Elemental HERO Neos is a powerful fusion monster and one of the strongest cards in Jaden's deck. It embodies his fighting spirit and determination. Jaden also uses various fusion strategies involving this card, including combining it with other "Elemental HERO" cards to form even more powerful monsters. Neos is a symbol of Jaden's signature playstyle and his connection to the "Elemental HERO" archetype.

Kaiba

Kaiba's signature card is the Blue-Eyes White Dragon. This card is iconic in both the Yu-Gi-Oh! anime and trading card game, representing Kaiba's strength and dominance as a duelist. The card symbolizes Kaiba's confidence, his rivalry with Yugi, and his drive to be the greatest duelist in the world. It is also featured in many of his duels, often as a key part of his strategy.

Yusei Fudo

Yusei Fudo's signature card is Stardust Dragon. Stardust Dragon is a powerful Synchro Monster with 2500 ATK and 2000 DEF, and it plays a central role in Yusei's deck throughout the Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's anime series. This card symbolizes Yusei's ideals of protecting his friends and fighting for justice.

Jack Atlas

Jack Atlas' signature card is Red Dragon Archfiend. Red Dragon Archfiend is a powerful Synchro Monster with 3000 ATK and 2000 DEF. It plays a central role in Jack's deck throughout the Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's anime series. The card represents Jack's intense ambition and desire for power, as well as his strong personality and pride as a duelist.

Yuma Tsukumo and Astral

Yuma Tsukumo and Astral's signature card is Number 39: Utopia. Number 39: Utopia is an Xyz Monster with 2500 ATK and 2000 DEF. It plays a central role in Yuma and Astral's dueling strategies throughout the Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL anime series. The card represents both Yuma's and Astral's growth as duelists and their bond throughout the series.

Kite Tenjo

Kaito Tenjo's signature card is Galaxy-Eyes Photon Dragon. Galaxy-Eyes Photon Dragon is a Level 8 Monster with 3000 ATK and 2500 DEF. It plays a key role in Kaito's deck throughout the Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL anime series and represents his strength and his connection to the "Photon" and "Galaxy" archetypes.

Yuya Sakaki

Yuya Sakaki's signature card is Odd-Eyes Dragon. Odd-Eyes Dragon is not only a strong card with 2500 ATK, but it also has special effects that allow Yuya to manipulate its attack power and even trigger additional effects during a duel. The card symbolizes Yuya's unique approach to dueling, blending entertainment and excitement with his battles.

Yusaku Fujiki

Decode Talker is a powerful Link Monster with 2300 ATK and 0 DEF. It plays a central role in Yusaku's deck throughout the Yu-Gi-Oh! VRAINS anime series. As Yusaku's signature monster, Decode Talker is a symbol of his growth and strategic approach to dueling. Decode Talker is the first Link Monster that Yusaku uses in the series and represents his skill and mastery over the new Link Summoning mechanic.
